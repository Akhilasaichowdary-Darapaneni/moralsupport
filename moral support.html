Numbers on a meter matter, but they are not the whole story. Children need to feel loved, safe, and understood as they live with diabetes. Parents and caregivers also need kindness and support.

Every check, injection, and sensor change is an act of bravery. Notice how far you have already come.

Stay Connected

Keep doing the things you enjoy: sports, art, music, games, and friends. Diabetes is something you manage, not who you are.
